My Background / About Me
Hello there! I'm Bastian Krohg, a Master’s student in International Management and Embedded Systems Engineering at Toulouse School of Management and INSA Toulouse. My journey includes an enriching exchange semester at Seoul National University and varied internships in Norway and Silicon Valley, contributing to my expertise in hardware and software engineering, and international business development.
I'm fluent in Norwegian, English, and French and proficient in a multitude of programming languages and technologies. My passion lies in leveraging technological innovation for impactful solutions.
